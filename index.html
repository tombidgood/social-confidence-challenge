Why I built this

A few years ago, I never imagined I'd become someone whose life revolves around communication.

Today, I'm a qualified journalist. I interview people for a living, speak to strangers every day, present on camera, appear on podcasts, and I've built an online platform through conversations and content.

But none of that came naturally.

It took thousands of real conversations to get here. Interviewing strangers, filming hundreds of videos, meeting people from every walk of life, and constantly pushing myself outside my comfort zone taught me what actually builds social confidence.

This 21-day challenge is everything I wish I'd had when I was first trying to become more confident socially.
